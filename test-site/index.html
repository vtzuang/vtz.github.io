Here is a recipe for pumpkin curry:

Ingredients needed:

  • pumpkin
  • spices
  • onions
  • noodles
  • olive oil

Steps to prepare:

  1. Microwave the pumpkin
  2. Scoop out the seeds
  3. Sautee onions
  4. Cube pumpkins and add to onions
  5. Add spices and noodles
  6. Enjoy!
